About CarbEmi-Metron

The name of our project "CarbEmi-Metron" is derived from 3 words - Carbon, Emissions, and Metron (a greek unit of measurement). These 3 words summarize our project perfectly. The core idea behind our project is to spread awareness about carbon emissions, greenhouse effect, and climate change. We strongly believe that giving people a way to calculate carbon emissions from a few of our everyday tasks will help them educate themselves and in theory, reduce the emissions as much as possible.

Motivation

Day by day, our climate is changing. We are experiencing more severe weather conditions like heat waves, draughts, tropical storms, flooding, etc. Despite this, there is very little awareness about the ill-effects of high carbon emissions. Everyone has learned about the greenhouse effect, the climate impacts, and the greenhouse gases in school, but we do not hear more about them when we talk about larger issues like pollution. In fact, a lot of people don't have sufficient idea about the harmful effects of carbon emissions. So, we decided to make a project to calculate the carbon emissions from a few of our every day activities.
